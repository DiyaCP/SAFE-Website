Cyber Bullying

Cyberbullying is an increasing concern in the digital age. Here are the key laws and actions that protect individuals from cyberbullying:

  • Information Technology Act, 2000 (Section 66A, 67): This section penalizes cyberstalking, online harassment, and sending obscene content via electronic communication.
  • Indian Penal Code (Section 354D, 507): These sections criminalize stalking, threatening messages, and other forms of harassment over the internet.

If you are a victim of cyberbullying, contact the police or the cybercrime helpline immediately.

For more information, visit the official website of the Ministry of Women and Child Development.
